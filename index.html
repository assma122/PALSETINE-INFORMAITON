PALESTINE

Palestine is sometimes called the Holy Land. It is a sacred place for three major religions: Judaism, Christianity, and Islam. Palestine is important to Jews because the ancient kingdom of Israel was located there. Jews believe that God promised the land to them.

Learn more about Palestine

PLACES:

  • Al-Aqsa Mosque is one of the most sacred sites in Jerusalem.
  • The Church of the Nativity in Bethlehem is believed to be the birthplace of Jesus Christ.
  • The Dome of the Rock is a famous Islamic landmark with a golden dome.

TRANSPORTS:

  1. Taxis are a popular way for people to get around.
  2. Buses are commonly used for traveling between cities and towns.
